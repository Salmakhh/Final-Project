Hello! My name is Salma Hamed and I'm an art lover.
I studied Art and Design in Helwan University, Egypt and am currently continuing my education studying Digital Media in West Valley College.
I work mainly with Oil and Acrylics on canvas with a few exceptions of Soft Pastels and Gouache on paper.
I hope you love my work as much as I love every piece. Feel free to contact me!
